Second Indication

In our story, we made mention of a Most Noble Commander and said that whoever is not blind and sees his decorations and medals will understand that he acts in accordance with the commands of a monarch and is his favoured servant .

The sacred Creator of so ornamented a cosmos must of necessity have a Noble Messenger , just as the sun must of necessity have light.

For it is impossible that the sun does not emit light, and Divinity cannot be without showing itself through the sending of prophets.

Is it at all possible that a beauty of utter perfection should not desire to manifest itself by means of one who will demonstrate and display it?
