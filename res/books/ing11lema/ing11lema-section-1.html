#11

Third Point: Almighty God has boundless love, just as He has infinite compassion.

Üçüncü Nokta: Cenab-ı Hakk'ın hadsiz merhameti olduğu gibi, hadsiz bir muhabbeti de vardır.

Just as He makes Himself loved in infinite fashion through all the fine qualities and adornment of the creatures in the universe,

Bütün kâinattaki masnûatın mehâsini ile ve süslendirmesiyle kendini hadsiz bir surette sevdirdiği gibi;

so He loves in particular conscious beings who respond with love to His making His artefacts loved.

masnuatını, hususan sevdirmesine sevmek ile mukabele eden zîşuur mahlûkatı sever.

It may be clearly understood how important and exalted an aim it is to try to attract the love of the Being one manifestation of whose mercy are all the subtle wonders and good things and delights and bounties of Paradise.

Cennet'in bütün letâif ve mehâsini ve lezâizi ve niamatı, bir cilve-i Rahmeti olan bir Zatın nazar-ı muhabbetini kendine celbe çalışmak, ne kadar mühim ve âlî bir maksad olduğu bilbedahe anlaşılır.

Since it is stated clearly that His love is won only through following Muhammad’s (UWBP) practices,

Madem nass-ı kelâmiyle; onun muhabbetine, yalnız ittiba-ı Sünnet-i Ahmediye (A.S.M.) ile mazhar olunur.

it is certain that it should be man’s greatest aim and his most urgent duty.

Elbette ittiba-ı Sünnet-i Ahmediye (A.S.M.), en büyük bir maksad-ı insanî ve en mühim bir vazife-i beşeriye olduğu tahakkuk eder.

ELEVENTH POINT

ONBİRİNCİ NÜKTE:

This consists of three matters.

"Üç Mes'ele"dir.

First Matter: The sources of the Noble Messenger’s (UWBP) illustrious practices are threefold:

Birinci Mes'ele: Resul-i Ekrem Aleyhissalâtü Vesselâm'ın Sünnet-i Seniyyesinin menbaı üçtür:

his words, his acts, and his conduct. And each of these has three categories: obligatory, voluntary, and laudable.

Akvâli, ef'ali, ahvâlidir. Bu üç kısım dahi, üç kısımdır: Ferâiz, nevâfil, âdât-ı hasenesidir.

It is imperative to follow those that are obligatory and compulsory,

Farz ve vâcib kısmında ittibaa mecburiyet var;

and penalties and punishment are incurred for giving them up.

terkinde, azab ve ikab vardır.

Everyone is charged with following them.

Herkes ona ittibaa mükelleftir.

As for the voluntary sort, as a means of winning God’s love, the believers are again charged with following them,

Nevafil kısmında, emr-i istihbâbî ile yine ehl-i îman mükelleftir.

but they receive no penalty if they fail to do so.

Fakat, terkinde azab ve ikab yoktur.

However, to act in accordance with them and to follow them is highly meritorious,

Fiilinde ve ittibaında azîm sevablar var

while to change them is innovation, misguidance, and a great error.

ve tağyir ve tebdili bid'a ve dalâlettir ve büyük hatadır.

To emulate the Prophet’s (UWBP) customary actions is extremely laudable and in accordance with wisdom, and is beneficial for both personal life and social life and for humankind.

Âdât-ı Seniyyesi ve harekât-ı müstahsenesi ise hikmeten, maslahaten, hayat-ı şahsiye ve nev'iye ve içtimaiye itibariyle onu taklid ve ittiba etmek, gâyet müstahsendir.

For all his habitual actions produce numerous things beneficial for life,

Çünki : Herbir hareket-i âdiyesinde, çok menfaat-ı hayatiye bulunduğu gibi,

and furthermore, by following them, such conduct and actions become worship.

mutabaat etmekle o âdâb ve âdetler, ibadet hükmüne geçer.

Yes, since as both friend and foe agree, the person of Muhammad (UWBP) manifested the highest degrees of moral virtues;

Evet madem dost ve düşmanın ittifakiyle, Zat-ı Ahmediye (A.S.M.) mehâsin-i ahlâkın en yüksek mertebelerine mazhardır.

and since as all are agreed, he is the most famous and excellent member of the human race;

Ve madem bil'ittifak nev-i beşer içinde en meşhur ve mümtaz bir şahsiyettir.

and since as is indicated by his thousands of miracles,

Ve madem binler mu'cizatın delâletiyle

and testified to by the World of Islam that he founded and its achievements,

ve teşkil ettiği Âlem-i İslâmiyetin ve kemalâtının şehadetiyle

and is affirmed by the truths of the Qur’an, which he heralded and interpreted,

ve mübelliğ ve tercüman olduğu Kur'an-ı Hakîm'in hakaikının tasdikiyle,

he was the most excellent Perfect Man and most excellent guide;

en mükemmel bir insan-ı kâmil ve bir mürşid-i ekmeldir.

and since as the fruit of following him, millions of the people of perfection have advanced through the degrees of attainment and reached the happiness of both worlds;

Ve madem semere-i ittibaiyle milyonlar ehl-i kemal, merâtib-i kemalâtta terakki edip saadet-i dâreyne vâsıl olmuşlardır.

for sure his practices and actions are the finest examples to be followed,

Elbette o zatın sünneti, harekâtı, iktida edilecek en güzel nümunelerdir

and the safest guides, and the soundest laws to be adopted as principles.

ve takib edilecek en sağlam rehberlerdir ve düstur ittihaz edilecek en muhkem kanunlardır.

Happy the person who has a large share of following the Prophet’s (UWBP) practices.

Bahtiyar odur ki, bu ittiba-ı Sünnette hissesi ziyade ola.

While those who are lazy and do not follow them suffer vast loss, and those who consider them to be unimportant commit a great crime,

Sünnete ittiba etmeyen, tenbellik eder ise, hasaret-i azîme; ehemmiyetsiz görür ise, cinayet-i azîme;

while if they criticize them, which infers denying them, it is serious misguidance.

tekzibini işmam eden tenkid ise, dalâlet-i azîmedir.

#12

Second Matter: Almighty God decrees in the All-Wise Qur’an,

İkinci Mes'ele: Cenab-ı Hak Kur'an-ı Hakîm'de:

And you [stand] on an exalted standard of character.(68:4)

َواِنَّكَ لَعَلَى خُلُقٍ عَظِيمٍ

ferman eder.

According to sound narrations, when the distinguished Companion ‘A’isha the Veracious (May God be pleased with her) described the Messenger (Upon whom be blessings and peace),

Rivayât-ı sahîha ile Hazret-i Âişe-i Sıddıka (R.A.) gibi sahabe-i güzin, Hazret-i Peygamber Aleyhissalâtü Vesselâm'ı tarif ettikleri zaman

she would say: “His character is the Qur’an.”

"Hulukuhu-l Kur'an" diye tarif ediyorlardı.

That is to say, Muhammad (Upon whom be blessings and peace) is the exemplar of the fine moral qualities described by the Qur’an.

Yâni: "Kur'anın beyan ettiği mehâsin-i ahlâkın misali, Muhammed Aleyhissalâtü Vesselâm'dır.

He conforms to them more than anyone, and his nature was created in accordance with them.

Ve o mehâsini en ziyade imtisal eden ve fıtraten o mehâsin üstünde yaratılan odur."

So even lunatics would understand how unfortunate are those heedless members of his community who believe in him yet give no importance to his practices or want to change them, despite each of his deeds, states, words, and actions being worthy of emulation by mankind.

İşte böyle bir zatın ef'al, ahvâl, akvâl ve harekâtının herbirisi, nev-i beşere birer model hükmüne geçmeye lâyık iken, ona îman eden ve ümmetinden olan gâfillerin, (sünnetine ehemmiyet vermeyen veyahût tağyir etmek istiyen) ne kadar bedbaht olduğunu divaneler de anlar.

Third Matter: Since the Noble Messenger (Upon whom be blessings and peace) was created with the most moderate character in the most perfect form,

Üçüncü Mes'ele: Resul-i Ekrem Aleyhissalâtü Vesselâm, hilkaten en mutedil bir vaziyette ve en mükemmel bir surette halkedildiğinden,

his actions and rest all proceeded on moderation and equanimity.

harekât ve sekenatı, itidal ve istikamet üzerine gitmiştir.

His biography shows clearly that he always acted with moderation and equanimity,

Siyer-i Seniyyesi, kat'î bir surette gösterir ki: Her hareketinde istikamet ve itidal üzerine gitmiş,

and avoided excess and negligence.

ifrat ve tefritten içtinab etmiştir.

Yes, God’s Noble Messenger (Upon whom be blessings and peace) conformed completely to the command:

Therefore stand firm [in the Straight Way] as you are commanded, (11:112)

Evet Resul-i Ekrem Aleyhissalâtü Vesselâm,

فَاسْتَقِمْ كَمَا اُمِرْتَ

emrini tamamiyle imtisal ettiği için,

and moderation is apparent in all his acts, words, and conduct.

bütün ef'al ve akvâl ve ahvâlinde istikamet, kat'î bir surette görünüyor.

For example, since his reasoning faculty was free of wiliness and stupidity, which are excess and deficiency – resembling its corruption and darkness,

Meselâ: Kuvve-i akliyenin fesad ve zulmeti hükmündeki ifrat ve tefriti olan gabavet ve cerbezeden müberra olarak,

it always functioned with wisdom, the middle way and means of moderation.

hadd-i vasat ve medâr-ı istikamet olan hikmet noktasında kuvve-i akliyesi daima hareket ettiği gibi;

Similarly, far from rage and cowardice, which are the corruption of the power of anger and its excess and deficiency,

kuvve-i gazabiyenin fesadı ve ifrat ve tefriti olan korkaklık ve tehevvürden münezzeh olarak,

his power of anger was always employed with sacred courage, which is the middle way and means of moderation of that power.

kuvve-i gadabiyenin medâr-ı istikameti ve hadd-i vasatı olan şecaat-ı kudsiye ile kuvve-i gadabiyesi hareket etmekle beraber;

And so too, purified of licentiousness and frigidity, which are the excess and deficiency of the power of animal appetites and its corruption,

kuvve-i şeheviyenin fesadı ve ifrat ve tefriti olan humud ve fücurdan musaffa olarak,

his power of passion always took chasteness, the means of moderation of that power, as guide, at the degree of maximum virtuousness. And so on.

o kuvvenin medâr-ı istikameti olan iffette, kuvve-i şeheviyesi daima iffeti, âzamî mâsumiyet derecesinde rehber ittihaz etmiştir. Ve hâkeza...

In all his practices, daily conduct, and in carrying out the injunctions of his Shari‘a, he chose the way of moderation

Bütün Sünen-i Seniyyesinde, ahvâl-i fıtriyesinde ve ahkâm-ı şer'iyyesinde, hadd-i istikameti ihtiyar edip

and avoided excess and deficiency, and wastefulness and prodigality, which are wrongdoing and darkness.

zulüm ve zulümat olan ifrat ve tefritten, israf ve tebzirden içtinab etmiştir.

He avoided wastefulness absolutely and took frugality as his guide in his speech even, and in eating and drinking.

Hatta tekellümünde ve ekl ve şürbünde, iktisadı rehber ve israftan kat'iyyen içtinab etmiştir.

Thousands of books have been written describing the details of this truth.

Bu hakikatın tafsilâtına dair binler cild kitab te'lif edilmiştir.

In accordance with “A hint is enough for the wise,”

اَلْعَارِفُ تَكْفِيهِ اْلاِشَارَةُ sırrınca,

we make do with this drop from the ocean and cut the story short here.

bu denizden bu katre ile iktifa edip, kıssayı kısa keseriz.

O God, grant blessings to he who brought together in his person all fine moral qualities, and manifested the mystery of, “And you [stand] on an exalted standard of character,” and who said:

اَللّهُمَّ صَلِّ عَلَى جَامِعِ مَكَارِمِ اْلاَخْلاَقِ وَ مَظْهَرِ سِرِّ (وَ اِنَّكَ لَعَلَى خُلُقٍ عَظِيمٍ) اَلَّذِى قَالَ

“Whoever adheres to my practices when my community is corrupted shall receive the reward of a hundred martyrs.”

مَنْ تَمَسَّكَ بِسُنَّتِى عِنْدَ فَسَادِ اُمَّتِى فَلَهُ اَجْرُ مِاَةِ شَهِيدٍ

And they shall say: “Praise be to God, who has guided us to this [felicity]; never could we have found guidance, had it not been for the guidance of God; indeed it was the truth that the prophets of our Sustainer brought to us.”(7:43)

وَ قَالُوا الْحَمْدُ اِللّهِ الَّذِى هَدينَا لِهذَا وَ مَا كُنَّا لَنَهْتَدِىَ لَوْ لاَ اَنْ هَدينَا اللّهُ لَقَدْ جَائَتْ رُسُلُ رَبِّنَا بِالْحَقِّ

Glory be unto You! We have no knowledge save that which You have taught us; indeed You are All-Knowing, All-Wise.(2:32)

سُبْحَانَكَ لاَ عِلْمَ لَنَا اِلاَّ مَا عَلَّمْتَنَا اِنَّكَ اَنْتَ الْعَلِيمُ الْحَكِيمُ
