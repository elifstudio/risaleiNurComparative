#31

Conclusion

Hâtime

O friend, whose mind is alert and heart, attentive!

Ey aklı hüşyar, kalbi müteyakkız arkadaş!

If you have understood this Twenty-Second Word from the beginning up to here, take the twelve Flashes together in your hand,

Eğer şu Yirmiikinci Söz'ün başından buraya kadar fehmetmişsen, Oniki Lem'ayı birden elinde tut.

and finding a lamp of truth as powerful as a thousand electric lamps, adhere to the below-mentioned verses of the Qur’an. Mount the steed of Divine assistance, ascend to the heavens of truth, rise to the Throne of Divine knowledge. Declare:

Binler elektrik kuvvetinde bir sirac-ı hakikat bularak, Arş-ı âzamdan uzatılıp gelen âyât-ı Kur'aniyeye yapış. Burak-ı tevfike bin, semâvât-ı hakaikte uruc et, Arş-ı mârifetullaha çık...

I testify that there is no god but You, You are One, You have no partner!37

37 Bukhari, i, 214; Ibn Hibban, Sahih, i, 272; iii, 136, 227, 229.

اَشْهَدُ اَنْ لآََ اِلهَ اِلآَّ اَنْتَ وَحْدَكَ لاَ شَرِيكَ لَكَ

de. Hem

And, saying,

I testify that there is no god but God, He is One, He has no partner; His is the dominion and His is the praise; He gives life and gives death, and He is ever- living and dies not; in His hand is all good, and He is powerful over all things,38

38 Baghawi, Sharh al-Sunna, v, 53, 75, 132; Ibn Hajar, Majma’ al-Zawa’id, iv, 86-115; x, 85, 114.

لآَ اِلَهَ اِلاَّ اللَّهُ وَحْدَهُ لاَ شَرِيكَ لَهُ لَهُ الْمُلْكُ وَ لَهُ الْحَمْدُ يُحْيِى وَ يُمِيتُ وَ هُوَ حَىٌّ لاَ يَمُوتُ بِيَدِهِ الْخَيْرُ وَ هُوَ عَلَى كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

proclaim His unity over the heads of all the beings in the universe in this mighty mosque of the world.

diyerek, bütün mevcûdât-ı kâinatın başları üstünde ve mescid-i kebir-i âlemde vahdaniyyeti ilân et...

Glory be to You! We have no knowledge save that which You have taught us;

indeed, You are All-Knowing, All-Wise.39

39 Qur’an, 2:32.

سُبْحَانَكَ لاَ عِلْمَ لَنَا آِلاَّ مَا عَلَّمْتَنَآ اِنَّكَ اَنْتَ االْعَلِيمُ الْحَكِيمُ

O our Sustainer! Do not call us to task if we forget or fall into error. * Our Sustainer! Lay not a burden on us like that which you laid on those before us; * Our Sustainer! Lay not on us a burden greater than we have the strength to bear. * Blot out our sins. And grant us forgiveness. Have mercy on us. You are our Protector; help us against those who stand against faith.40

40 Qur’an, 2:286.

رَبَّنَا لاَ تُؤَاخِذْنَآ اِنْ نَسِينَا اَوْ اَخْطَاْنَا رَبَّنَا وَلاَ تَحْمِلْ عَلَيْنَآ اِصْرًا كَمَا حَمَلْتَهُ عَلَى الَّذِينَ مِنْ قَبْلِنَا رَبَّنَا وَلاَ تُحَمِّلْنَا مَا لاَ طَاقَةَ لَنَا بِهِ وَاعْفُ عَنَّا وَاغْفِرْلَنَا وَارْحَمْنَا اَنْتَ مَوْلَينَا فَانْصُرْنَا عَلَى الْقَوْمِ االْكَافِرِينَ

Our Sustainer! Let not our hearts deviate now after You have guided us, but grant us mercy from Your presence; for You are the Granter of bounties without measure. * Our Sustainer! You are He that will gather mankind together against a Day about which there is no doubt; for God never fails in His promise.41

41 Qur’an, 3:8-9.

رَبَّنَا لاَ تُزِغْ قُلُوبَنَا بَعْدَ اِذْ هَدَيْتَنَا وَهَبْ لَنَا مِنْ لَدُنْكَ رَحْمَةً اِنَّكَ اَنْتَ الْوَهَّابُ رَبَّنَا اِنَّكَ جَامِعُ النَّاسِ لِيَوْمٍ لاَ رَيْبَ فِيهِ اِنَّ اللَّهَ لاَ يُخْلِفُ الْمِيعَادَ

O God! Grant blessings and peace to the one whom You sent as a Mercy to all the worlds, and to all his Family and Companions. And have mercy of us and have mercy on his community, through Your mercy, O Most Merciful of the Merciful. Amen.

اَللَّهُمَّ صَلِّ وَ سَلِّمْ عَلَى مَنْ اَرْسَلْتَهُ رَحْمَةً لِلْعَالَمِينَ وَ عَلَى اَلِهِ وَ صَحْبِهِ اَجْمَعِينَ وَارْحَمْنَا وَ ارْحَمْ اُمَّتَهُ بِرَحْمَتِكَ يَا اَرْحَمَ الرَّاحِمِينَ اَمِينَ

And the close of their prayer will be: Praise be to God, the Sustainer of All the Worlds! 42

42 Qur’an, 10:10.

وَ اَخِرُ دَعْوَيهُمْ اَنِ الْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ الْعَالَمِينَ
