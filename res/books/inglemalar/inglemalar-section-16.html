For clear evidence of the pleasure to be found in the performance of duties consider your own members and emotions.

Vazifede lezzet bulunduğuna en zahir bir delil, sen kendi aza ve duygularının hizmetlerine bak.

Each receives different pleasures in performing the duties connected to your personal survival and the survival of the human race.

Her biri beka-i şahsî ve beka-i nev'î için ettikleri hizmetlerinde ayrı ayrı lezzetleri var.

The duties themselves are a means of enjoyment for them, and to give up a duty is a sort of torment for a member.
