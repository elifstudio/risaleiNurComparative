Regretfully, our secret dissembling enemies sometimes attach the name of Sufism to the reality of Islam, which has been gained and preserved through the swords and blood of the hundred million martyrs at the rank of saints, and heroic war veterans of this religious nation.

Maatteessüf gizli münafık düşmanlarımız, bu dindar milletin yüzer milyon veli makamında olan şehidlerinin, kahraman gazilerinin kanıyla ve kılıncıyla kazanılan ve muhafaza edilen hakikat-ı İslâmiyete bazen "tarîkat" namını takıp

While the way of Sufism is only a single ray of that sun, they show it to be the sun and deceive certain lax government officials.

hakikat-ı Kur'aniyeye ve hakaik-i îmaniyeye tesirli bir surette çalışan Nur talebelerine "tarîkatçı" ve "siyasî cem'iyetçi" namını vererek aleyhimize sevketmek istiyorlar.

“Let us too be sacrificed for this sacred truth for which hundreds of millions of people have been sacrificed!

and with respect to merit, each transient day spent in illness and under oppression gains ten days of perpetual life.
