First Point

Birinci Nükte

Just as the Qur’an of Miraculous Exposition expresses truths through its explicit, clear meanings and senses,

Each of its verses contains numerous levels of meanings.

Her bir âyetin çok tabaka-i mânâları var.

The Qur’an proceeds from all-encompassing knowledge, so all its meanings may be intended.

Kur'an, ilm-i muhitten geldiği için, bütün mânâları murad olabilir.

Its meanings are not restricted to one or two like man’s speech, the product of his limited mind and individual will.

İnsanın cüz'î fikri ve şahsî iradesiyle olan kelâmlar gibi bir iki mânâya inhisar etmez.

In consequence of this, innumerable truths contained in the Qur’an’s verses have been expounded by Qur’anic commentators,

İşte bu sırra binaen Âyât-ı Kur'aniyenin ehl-i tefsir tarafından hadsiz hakaiki beyan edilmiş.

Müfessirînin beyan etmediği daha çok hakaiki var.

In addition to its explicit meanings, much significant knowledge is contained in its letters in particular and in its allusions.

Ve bilhassa hurufatında ve mânâ-yı sârihinden başka, işârâtında çok ulûm-u mühimme vardır.

Conclusion

Hâtime

A miraculous, subtle point of the All-Wise Qur’an that is manifested through
‘coincidences’* is as follows:

Kur'an-ı Hakîm'in tevâfuk cihetinden tezâhür eden i'cazî nüktelerinden bir nüktesi şudur ki:

*{‘Coincidence’ (tevâfuk): the correspondence of letters or words in lines or patterns on one or several pages. (Tr.)}

In the All-Wise Qur’an, the divine names of Allah, Merciful (Rahman), Compassionate (Rahim), Sustainer (Rabb), and He (Hu) in place of Allah, are mentioned approximately four thousand times in all.

Kur'an-ı Hakîm'de İsm-i Allah, Rahmân, Rahîm,
Rab ve İsm-i Celâl yerindeki Hüve'nin mecmuu, dörtbin küsurdur.

The second type of abjad, reckoned according to the arrangement of the alphabet, makes the value of “In the Name of God, the Merciful, the Compassionate” around four thousand also.

بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرّحْمنِ الرّحِيمِ (Hesab-ı Ebced'in ikinci nev'i ki, huruf-u heca tertibiyledir) o da dörtbin küsur eder.

Small fractions of large numbers do not spoil ‘coincidence’, so have been disregarded.

Büyük adedlerde küçük kesirler, tevâfuku bozmadığından küçük kesirlerden kat-ı nazar edildi.

In addition to coinciding with both the approximately two hundred and eighty instances of the word Allah in Sura al-Baqara, and the around two hundred and eighty verses of the Sura,

Aynen Sure-i El-Bakara'nın ikiyüz seksen küsur İsm-i Celâline ve hem ikiyüz seksen küsur âyâtın adedine tevâfuk etmekle beraber,

if reckoned with the second sort of abjad reckoning, it again makes about four thousand.

That coincides both with the five famous divine names mentioned above,

O da yukarıda zikri geçmiş beş Esmâ-i meşhûrenin adedine tevâfuk etmekle beraber

and if the fractions are disregarded, with the numerical value of “In the Name of God, the Merciful, the Compassionate.”

بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرّحِيمِ in kesirlerinden kat-ı nazar, adedine tevâfuk ediyor.

hem El-Bakara'ya isim, hem Kur'ana isim,

and a summary of “In the Name of God, the Merciful, the Compassionate.”

hem بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرّحِيمِ in mücmelidir.

According to the well-known Abjad system, “In the Name of God, the Merciful, the Compassionate” is equal in numerical value to the name of Sustainer.

Ebced'in meşhur hesabıyla بِسْمِ اللّهِ الرَّحْمَنِ الرّحِيمِ ism-i Rab adedine müsavi olmakla beraber,

Similarly, if the doubled (Ra) in “the Merciful, the Compassionate (ar-Rahman, ar-Rahim)” is counted twice, it becomes nine hundred and ninety

and a key to numerous important mysteries – with its nineteen letters, the key to nineteen thousand worlds.

ondokuz harfiyle ondokuz bin âlemin miftahıdır.

seksen tevâfuk olup, o satırın nısf-ı evvelindeki seksen tevâfuka ve nısf-ı âhirdeki yine seksen tevâfuka tevâfuk ediyor.
